<p><p>The tunnel metal detector market is primarily segmented into two types: magnetic metal detection and non-magnetic metal detection. Magnetic metal detectors utilize magnetic fields to identify ferrous metals, making them effective for detecting items like iron and steel. In contrast, non-magnetic metal detectors employ techniques such as electromagnetic induction to detect non-ferrous metals like aluminum and copper. Each type serves specific applications across industries like security, mining, and recycling, catering to diverse detection needs.</p></p>
