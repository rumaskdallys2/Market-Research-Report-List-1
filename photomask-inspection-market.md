<p><strong>The Photomask Inspection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical</li><li>E-beam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photomask inspection market comprises two primary types: optical and e-beam inspection systems. Optical inspection utilizes light to detect defects on photomasks, offering high-speed analysis and high-throughput capabilities, making it suitable for production environments. In contrast, e-beam inspection employs electron beams for higher resolution imaging, allowing for the identification of smaller defects but operating at lower speeds. Both methods are crucial for ensuring photomask quality in semiconductor manufacturing, enhancing the reliability of integrated circuits and devices.</p></p>
