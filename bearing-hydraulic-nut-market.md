<p><p>The Bearing Hydraulic Nut market consists of three primary types: Shim Type Nuts, Lower Collar Nuts, and Upper Collar Nuts. Shim Type Nuts utilize shims for spacing and precise torque application, ensuring accurate assembly. Lower Collar Nuts are designed for stability and are placed beneath bearings, distributing load effectively. Upper Collar Nuts secure the top of bearing assemblies, providing essential support and alignment. Each type plays a critical role in enhancing the performance and reliability of machinery in various industrial applications.</p></p>
