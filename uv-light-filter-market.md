<p><ul><li>"Organic Filter"</li><li>"Inorganic Filter"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV light filter market is categorized into two primary types: organic filters and inorganic filters. Organic filters are derived from carbon-based compounds, offering effective UV absorption and typically featuring a lighter weight and flexible applications. In contrast, inorganic filters are made from mineral-based substances like metal oxides, known for their durability and superior opacity against UV radiation. Both types are utilized across industries such as cosmetics, automotive, and industrial coatings, providing essential protection against harmful UV rays.</p></p>
