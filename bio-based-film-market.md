<p><ul><li>Compostable Films</li><li>Non-compostable Films</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bio-based film market is divided into two main types: compostable films and non-compostable films. Compostable films are designed to break down into organic matter under specific composting conditions, making them environmentally friendly and suitable for applications like food packaging. Non-compostable films, while still derived from bio-based materials, do not decompose in composting environments and may have applications in diverse sectors, including agriculture and durable goods, but retain a longer lifecycle, requiring different end-of-life management.</p></p>
