<p><ul><li>Hot Wire Airflow Sensor</li><li>Vane Airflow Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mass air flow (MAF) sensor market encompasses various technologies, primarily the hot wire airflow sensor and the vane airflow sensor. Hot wire sensors utilize a heated wire element to measure air velocity based on changes in temperature, offering high accuracy and rapid response. Conversely, vane airflow sensors operate using a mechanical vane that adjusts to airflow, providing a simpler, more robust solution but often with lower sensitivity. Both types are critical in automotive and industrial applications for optimizing engine performance and efficiency.</p></p>
