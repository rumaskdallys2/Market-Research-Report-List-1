<p><p>The electric vehicle battery separator market comprises different types based on layers. Single-layer separators, such as polypropylene (PP) and polyethylene (PE), offer basic functionality for battery performance. Bilayer separators, like PP/PE and PP/PP, enhance safety and efficiency by combining materials for improved ionic conductivity and thermal stability. Trilayer separators (PP/PE/PP) provide advanced features, optimizing performance through a combination of materials that improve mechanical strength and electrochemical stability, making them suitable for high-performance electric vehicle batteries.</p></p>
