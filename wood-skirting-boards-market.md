<p><p>The wood skirting boards market comprises three main types based on density: Low Density Fiberboard (LDF), Medium-Density Fiberboard (MDF), and High Density Fiberboard (HDF). LDF is lightweight and cost-effective, suitable for low-traffic areas. MDF offers a balance of durability and affordability, making it popular for residential use. HDF is the densest, providing superior strength and durability, ideal for high-traffic environments. Each type serves specific applications, catering to varying aesthetic and functional requirements in interior design.</p></p>
