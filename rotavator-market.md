<p><p>The rotavator market consists mainly of two types: horizontal axis and vertical axis rotavators. Horizontal axis rotavators operate with blades positioned parallel to the ground, efficiently tilling the soil while minimizing surface disruption. In contrast, vertical axis rotavators feature blades oriented perpendicular to the ground, allowing deeper penetration and thorough soil mixing. Both types serve various agricultural applications, enhancing soil aeration, improving crop yield, and reducing manual labor, thus catering to diverse farming needs and preferences.</p></p>
