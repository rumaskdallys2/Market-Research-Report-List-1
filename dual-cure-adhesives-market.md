In contrast, epoxy resin-based adhesives also employ dual curing methods, providing superior durability and chemical resistance, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, construction, and electronics, enhancing bonding performance across various materials.</p></p>
