<p><p>Transfer pumps for the semiconductor market are essential for handling chemicals and other fluids during manufacturing processes. Diaphragm pumps operate with a flexible membrane, offering durability and chemical resistance. Peristaltic pumps use rollers to compress a tube, ensuring a leak-free transfer. Magnetic pumps employ magnetic fields for contactless fluid movement, enhancing safety. Bellows pumps utilize flexible bellows for precise control and low pulsation. Other types may include screw pumps and gear pumps, catering to specific operational requirements.</p></p>
