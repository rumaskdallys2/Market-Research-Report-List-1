<p><strong>The Automotive Radiator Reservoir Tank Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressurized</li><li>Non-pressurized</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive radiator reservoir tank market is categorized into two main types: pressurized and non-pressurized systems. Pressurized reservoir tanks can withstand higher temperatures and pressures, preventing coolant evaporation and ensuring optimal engine performance. They are commonly used in modern vehicles. In contrast, non-pressurized tanks operate at atmospheric pressure and are typically found in older or simpler designs. While they are less complex and cost-effective, they may be less efficient in maintaining coolant dynamics under high-temperature conditions.</p></p>
