<p><p>The drone goggles market is primarily divided into two types: box goggles and low profile goggles. Box goggles, characterized by their larger size, offer a more immersive viewing experience due to their enhanced field of vision and integrated displays. They are often preferred for long-range and high-performance flying. In contrast, low profile goggles are compact and lightweight, making them suitable for racing and outdoor activities. These models prioritize portability without compromising on video quality, catering to different user preferences and applications.</p></p>
