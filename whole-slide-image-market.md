<p><ul><li>Brightfield</li><li>Fluorescence</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Whole Slide Imaging (WSI) encompasses various market types, primarily Brightfield and Fluorescence imaging. Brightfield imaging utilizes transmitted light for visualizing stained tissue samples, offering clarity for standard diagnostics. In contrast, Fluorescence imaging employs specific wavelengths to excite fluorescent dyes in samples, revealing molecular details and enabling advanced analysis of cellular components. Both types cater to different research and clinical needs, with applications in pathology, cancer research, and personalized medicine, facilitating enhanced diagnostic accuracy and research capabilities in life sciences.</p></p>
