<p><p>The Copper Foil for Printed Circuit Board market primarily includes two types: Rolled Copper Foil and Electrolytic Copper Foil. Rolled Copper Foil is produced through mechanical processes, offering excellent thickness uniformity and flexibility, ideal for high-frequency applications. In contrast, Electrolytic Copper Foil is made via an electrochemical process, resulting in a smoother surface and superior conductivity, making it suitable for dense circuitry and advanced technology. Both types are crucial for enhancing the performance and reliability of PCBs.</p></p>
