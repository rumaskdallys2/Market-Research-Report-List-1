<p><p>The Medical Grade β-Tricalcium Phosphate (β-TCP) market is segmented into two primary types: Amorphous and Spherical. Amorphous β-TCP is characterized by its non-crystalline structure, offering enhanced bioactivity and faster resorption rates, making it suitable for various applications in bone regeneration. Spherical β-TCP, on the other hand, features a defined spherical shape, which allows for improved flowability and packing density in medical formulations. Both types play significant roles in orthopedic and dental procedures, catering to different clinical needs.</p></p>
