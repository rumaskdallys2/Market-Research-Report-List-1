<p><ul><li>SiO/C</li><li>Si/C</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicon anode materials market primarily consists of two types: silicon oxide/carbon (SiO/C) and silicon/carbon (Si/C) composites. SiO/C materials combine silicon oxide with carbon to enhance electrical conductivity and improve the cycling stability of lithium-ion batteries. Conversely, Si/C composites utilize silicon particles coated or mixed with carbon, offering high energy density and capacity while maintaining structural integrity during charge cycles. Both types contribute to the evolving landscape of advanced battery technologies aimed at higher performance and efficiency.</p></p>
