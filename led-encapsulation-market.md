<p><p>The LED encapsulation market comprises three main types: epoxy, silicone, and polyurethane. Epoxy encapsulation is known for its durability and excellent adhesion properties, making it suitable for harsh environments. Silicone provides flexibility and high-temperature resistance, often used in applications requiring thermal stability. Polyurethane offers a balance of flexibility and toughness, ideal for various applications where impact resistance is needed. Each material caters to specific requirements, influencing performance, longevity, and application suitability in LED technology.</p></p>
