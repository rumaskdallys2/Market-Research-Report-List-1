<p><p>The cooling water hydraulic pump market includes piston and positive-displacement types. Piston pumps utilize a reciprocating motion to move fluid and are known for high pressure and efficiency. Positive-displacement pumps, including gear and diaphragm models, continuously move fluid by trapping a fixed amount and displacing it through the outlet. Both types are vital in industrial applications where accurate flow control and reliability are required, ensuring efficient cooling and system performance in various hydraulic systems.</p></p>
