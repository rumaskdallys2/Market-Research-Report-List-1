<p><p>The Exhaust Gas Treatment System market comprises several technologies aimed at reducing emissions. Wet scrubbers employ liquid solutions to capture pollutants from exhaust gases, effectively reducing particulate matter and chemical pollutants. Electrostatic filtration uses charged plates to attract and remove particles from the airflow. Rotary dust removal systems utilize centrifugal force to segregate dust from gas streams, enhancing air quality and meeting environmental regulations.</p></p>
