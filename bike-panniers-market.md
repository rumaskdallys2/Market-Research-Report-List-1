<p><p>The bike panniers market is primarily categorized into two types: PVC and nylon. PVC panniers offer waterproof properties and durability, making them ideal for harsh weather conditions and heavy loads. They are often more rigid and easier to clean. On the other hand, nylon panniers are lighter and typically more breathable, providing flexibility and ease of storage. They may be treated for water resistance but may not offer the same level of protection as PVC. Both materials cater to various cycling preferences and needs.</p></p>
