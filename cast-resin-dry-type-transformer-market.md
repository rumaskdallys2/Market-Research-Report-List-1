<p><p>The Cast Resin Dry-Type Transformer market primarily features two design types: Concentric and Overlap. Concentric Type transformers have a cylindrical design where the windings are arranged concentrically, offering improved electrical performance and thermal management. Overlap Type transformers, on the other hand, have windings arranged in an overlapping manner, which enhances the magnetic coupling and reduces losses. Both types are favored for their durability, safety, and efficiency, making them suitable for various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
