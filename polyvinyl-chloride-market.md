<p><p>The Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) market is divided into two primary types: rigid PVC and flexible PVC. Rigid PVC is primarily used in construction applications, such as pipes, profiles, and fittings, due to its durability and strength. Conversely, flexible PVC is more adaptable and is used in products like flooring, medical devices, and wire insulation, owing to its elasticity and versatility. Both types cater to diverse industries, reflecting PVC's significance in various applications from infrastructure to consumer goods.</p></p>
